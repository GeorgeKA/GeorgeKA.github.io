Bachelor of Engineering - Mechatronics Engineering

Starting in September 2010 I began my undergrad in mechatronics engineering at McMaster University.
After 4 years and internship at Blackberry I successfully completed the program in April 2015.

What is mechatronics engineering anyway? Put simply, it is the design of computer controlled electromechanical systems.
